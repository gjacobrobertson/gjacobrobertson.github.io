Scrap

Scrap is a simple shared cost utility I built for me and my roommates. Just set up a group of friends, and when one person pays for something thats supposed to be split amongst the group, enter it on the site and it will keep track of all of the debts within your group of friends.

What The Fuck Should We Play?

What The Fuck Should We Play is a minimalist web application for determining what board game to play out of a given collection. It requires that you have an account with boardgamegeek.com to work. Just enter your boardgamegeek.com username, and the number of players you are looking for a game for, and it will tell you what the fuck to play. Rate the games in your BGG collection for better results.
